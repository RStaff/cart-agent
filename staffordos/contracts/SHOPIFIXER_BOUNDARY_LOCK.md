# SHOPIFIXER BOUNDARY LOCK

## 1. Active product
ShopiFixer is the only active merchant-facing product for outreach, audits, and follow-up.

## 2. Abando status
Abando is backend-only and may not appear in public copy, routes, metadata, CTAs, email content, or result pages.

## 3. Public base
All live outreach links must originate from the single approved public base:
https://staffordmedia.ai

## 4. Approved merchant-facing paths
Only approved ShopiFixer paths may be used in outreach.
No localhost, ngrok, dev port, or unsupported route may ever be sent to a lead.

## 5. Pre-send gate
Before any live send:
- open the exact link manually
- confirm HTTP 200
- confirm ShopiFixer branding only
- confirm no Abando strings
- confirm no dev/tunnel URLs
- confirm sender identity
- confirm exact subject/body/html

## 6. Route ownership
Every live outreach link must have:
- one canonical builder
- one canonical rendered destination
- one named code owner path

## 7. No mixed product messaging
ShopiFixer may promise audits, review, strongest issue, and next fix.
It may not promise Abando installs, Abando dashboard access, recovery agent setup, or embedded app flows.

## 8. Traceability
Every email send must be traceable by:
- recipient
- sender
- subject
- rendered destination link
- provider response
- timestamp
- queue stage

## 9. Runtime independence
No live customer-facing link may depend on a local tunnel, local process, or laptop state.

## 10. Priority
Ship ShopiFixer first.
Abando remains on the back burner until ShopiFixer is stable, traceable, and capable of producing revenue.
