# SHOPIFIXER REVENUE CAPACITY LOCK V1

## Revenue Target

Initial target:

2-5 ShopiFixer closes per day.

Price:

$950 flat fee per ShopiFixer fix.

## Daily Revenue Range

2 closes/day = $1,900/day
5 closes/day = $4,750/day

## Weekly Revenue Range

Assuming 5 selling days:

$9,500/week to $23,750/week

## Critical Constraint

The business cannot scale closes faster than it can deliver proof-backed outcomes.

## Current Bottleneck

Not checkout.
Not Stripe.
Not packet creation.

Current bottleneck:

Paid packet -> execution -> proof package -> merchant satisfaction

## Required Before Scaling

1. Complete one controlled real payment validation.
2. Prove packet transitions to payment_received.
3. Fulfill one paid packet manually.
4. Produce one proof package.
5. Confirm merchant-facing proof is credible.
6. Then repeat before automating further.

## Do Not Drift Into

- New AI features
- More dashboards
- Abando expansion
- Outreach scaling before fulfillment proof
- Claims of conversion lift without evidence

## Success Principle

2-5 daily closes only matters if each close can be fulfilled with quality, proof, and merchant trust.
