# SHOPIFIXER DAILY SALES CHECKLIST V1

## Daily Objective

Find and contact merchants likely to buy one $950 ShopiFixer Fix Sprint.

## Minimum Daily Motion

1. Identify 5 Shopify stores.
2. Inspect each store for one visible friction point.
3. Write one specific note per store.
4. Send outreach.
5. Track response.
6. If interested, send $950 checkout link.
7. After payment, verify packet state before work.

## Store Qualification

A good prospect has:

- Live Shopify store
- Real products
- Visible cart/product/checkout friction
- Owner or decision maker reachable
- Enough revenue potential that $950 is reasonable

## Disqualify

- Broken stores with no traffic
- Hobby stores with no clear revenue
- Stores where no visible fix is obvious
- Merchants likely to need a full rebuild instead of one sprint

## Outreach Rule

Specific beats generic.

Do not say:
"I can help with your Shopify store."

Say:
"I noticed [specific issue] that may be causing checkout friction."

## Close Rule

Do not overexplain.

Ask:
"Would you like me to send the $950 Fix Sprint checkout link?"

## After Payment Rule

Do not start work until:
payment_received is confirmed in packet authority.
