# SHOPIFIXER AUDIT AUTHORITY V1

## Purpose

Convert merchant trust into payment by identifying one specific, visible, explainable friction issue.

## Audit Output

Every ShopiFixer audit must contain:

1. Store Reviewed
2. Affected Page URL
3. Screenshot of Current State
4. Observed Friction
5. Why It Matters
6. Proposed Fix
7. Mockup or Annotated Screenshot
8. Expected Improvement Category
9. Sprint Scope
10. Flat Fee Offer

## Improvement Categories

- Trust
- Product Clarity
- Cart Friction
- Checkout Friction
- Mobile Usability
- Navigation
- Shipping Clarity
- Returns Clarity
- Visual Hierarchy

## Merchant Question

Why should I pay $950?

## Audit Answer

Because the issue is:

- visible
- explainable
- fixable
- provable

and the sprint includes:

- implementation
- QA
- before/after proof

## Pass Criteria

A merchant can review the audit and understand:

- what the issue is
- why it matters
- what will change
- what they are buying

without needing a call.

## Anti-Drift Rule

Do not add AI complexity.

Do not add large reports.

The audit exists to create trust and justify the sprint.
