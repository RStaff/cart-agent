# Shopifixer Outreach Pack V1

## ICP Definition

Target Shopify stores doing roughly $10k-$200k per month that are large enough to buy a small focused service and small enough to move quickly.

## Who To Target

- Founder-led Shopify brands
- Small ecommerce teams
- Brands with active paid or social traffic
- Stores with visible storefront friction
- Stores with enough product margin to justify a $99 next step

## Who To Avoid

- Enterprise or procurement-heavy brands
- Non-Shopify stores
- Agencies asking for free teardown work
- Stores that already show a clearly polished lifecycle stack
- Hobby stores with no visible commercial activity

## Manual Sourcing Methods

- Google search by niche plus Shopify indicators
- Instagram brand pages linked to Shopify stores
- TikTok brands sending traffic to storefronts
- LinkedIn or founder posts discussing growth or conversion
- Shopify ecosystem browsing by niche or app usage clues

## Execution Flow

1. Find targets manually.
2. Send a first-touch message.
3. Track the touch.
4. Wait for response.
5. Send audit link only after interest.
6. Close to the $99 fix plan.
7. Deliver.
8. Log what happened.

## Rules

- Do not send audit first.
- Speed over over-research.
- Goal = first sale, not scale.
- Keep the message specific enough to feel real.
- Do not fake personalization.
