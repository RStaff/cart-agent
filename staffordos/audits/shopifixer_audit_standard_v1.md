# SHOPIFIXER AUDIT STANDARD V1

## Purpose

Create a professional-grade assessment that builds trust and justifies a ShopiFixer Fix Sprint.

The audit is a sales asset.

The sprint is the product.

---

## Required Sections

### 1. Executive Summary

Summarize:

- store reviewed
- overall observations
- primary opportunity
- recommended sprint

---

### 2. Store Assessment Scorecard

Score:

- Trust
- Product Clarity
- Product Discovery
- Checkout Readiness
- Mobile Experience

Scale:

1-10

---

### 3. Findings Inventory

Document:

- finding
- affected page
- severity
- business rationale

Minimum:

3 findings

Target:

5 findings

---

### 4. Sprint Recommendation

Select ONE issue.

Explain:

- why selected
- why now
- why not the other findings

---

### 5. Before-State Evidence

Include:

- screenshot
- annotation
- explanation

Evidence must be visible.

---

### 6. Proposed Fix

Document:

- change
- scope
- implementation plan

Must be specific.

---

### 7. Expected Impact

Describe likely improvements in:

- trust
- clarity
- usability
- product discovery
- checkout flow

Do not guarantee conversion lift.

---

### 8. Proof Plan

Describe:

- before evidence
- implementation
- QA
- after evidence
- proof package

---

### 9. Sprint Deliverables

Merchant receives:

- implementation
- QA
- before/after proof
- completion summary

---

## Quality Rule

Every audit must answer:

1. What is wrong?
2. Why does it matter?
3. Why was this issue selected?
4. What exactly will change?
5. How will success be proven?

---

## Anti-Drift Rule

Do not create large consulting reports.

The audit exists to create trust, justify the sprint, and accelerate payment.
