## Observed Issue

Homepage hero section dominates the page while product discovery is pushed below the fold.

Affected Page:
Homepage

Category:
Product Clarity / Conversion Friction

---

## Evidence

The first screen is heavily brand-focused.

A visitor sees:

- brand statement
- hero image
- ENTER THE FORGE button

The visitor does not immediately see:

- product benefits
- pricing
- trust indicators
- product details

Products are pushed further down the page.

---

## Why It Matters

New visitors may not immediately understand:

- what is being sold
- why it is different
- why they should buy now

This can create hesitation before product exploration.

---

## Proposed Sprint Fix

Add a product-focused value section directly below the hero.

Potential elements:

- product benefit summary
- featured product callout
- trust statement
- clearer path to first product
