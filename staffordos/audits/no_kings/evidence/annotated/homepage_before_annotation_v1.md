## Observed Friction

The homepage first impression is brand-heavy, while product discovery and product value are delayed.

The visitor sees the brand world first, but may not immediately see:

- what specific products are available
- why the products are different
- what the first buying path should be
- trust or purchase confidence signals
- a product-focused reason to continue

---

## Why It Matters

A new visitor may hesitate before reaching the products.

If the homepage does not quickly clarify the product offer, the visitor has to work harder to understand what to buy and why.

This is a product clarity and discovery issue, not a full-site redesign issue.

---

## Proposed Fix Area

Add or improve an early product-focused value section near the top of the homepage.

The section should help the visitor understand:

- what is being sold
- what makes it valuable
- where to go next
- why the store is trustworthy enough to continue

---

## Expected After-State Proof

After implementation, the same desktop and mobile homepage captures should show:

- clearer product value earlier in the page
- more obvious path to product exploration
- improved product discovery without removing the brand identity
- desktop and mobile layout remaining coherent
