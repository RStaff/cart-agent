The proof must show a visible, merchant-understandable change.

For the current No Kings issue, the before/after proof should show:

- Before: the hero dominates the first screen and product value/products are visually delayed.
- After: visitors get a clearer product path or product value signal earlier in the page.
- Before: the merchant can see the friction without trusting an opinion.
- After: the merchant can compare the improved state against the same page context.
- QA: desktop and mobile views remain usable and coherent.

The proof does not need to show revenue lift to justify the sprint. It must show the paid work was specific, completed, and visible.
