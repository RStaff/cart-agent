## Positioning: Governed Conversion Intervention

The Fix Sprint is positioned as a **Governed Conversion Intervention**.

Definition:

One prioritized customer-journey problem addressed through a bounded group of
coordinated storefront changes, including diagnosis, implementation, QA,
before/after evidence, rollback protection, proof package, and measurement plan.

This positioning strengthens how the same $950 Fix Sprint is described. It does
not change the price, does not invent outcomes, and does not expand scope.

- "One prioritized customer-journey problem" is the same single scoped issue the
  Fix Sprint has always addressed — now named as a journey problem, not a cosmetic
  defect.
- "A bounded group of coordinated storefront changes" clarifies that resolving one
  journey problem may require more than a single edit, while remaining bounded. It
  is not, and never becomes, a full redesign.
- "Governed" means every change is diagnosed, implemented, QA'd, evidenced,
  rollback-protected, proven, and paired with a measurement plan — the same
  discipline already proven in StaffordOS mission governance.

## Measurement Plan

Every Governed Conversion Intervention ships with a measurement plan.

The measurement plan defines:

- what will be measured for the resolved journey problem
- how before/after evidence is compared
- what signal indicates the change is working

The measurement plan does not guarantee revenue and does not promise a specific
conversion or revenue outcome. It defines how the merchant can observe the change.

## Preserved Boundaries

The Governed Conversion Intervention positioning preserves, without exception:

- **$950 flat fee** — the price is unchanged.
- **One scoped problem** — one prioritized customer-journey problem per sprint.
- **No full redesign** — a bounded group of coordinated changes, never a rebuild.
- **No retainer** — the Fix Sprint is a one-time engagement, not ongoing.
- **No guaranteed revenue** — a measurement plan is provided; no revenue or
  conversion outcome is promised.
- **No unbounded revisions** — the scope is bounded; revisions are not unlimited.
- **ShopiFixer and Abando remain separate products** — the Fix Sprint is never
  bundled with, merged into, or sold as Abando.
