# SHOPIFIXER PATTERN 001
# Homepage Product Value Path

## Problem

The homepage has a strong brand hero, but the first clear buying path is weak or delayed.

## Use When

- Hero is visually strong
- Product discovery appears below the hero
- Product section is generic, weak, delayed, or unclear
- Trust support appears too late
- Visitor may not know what to buy first

## Fix Pattern

Preserve the hero.

Improve the first product decision directly after the hero.

Add or improve:

- product value headline
- short product explanation
- real product image
- product title
- correct price
- primary CTA
- secondary CTA
- verified trust microcopy

## Standard Section Order

Header
Hero
Product Value Section
Trust Support
Featured Products
Brand Story
Email Capture
Footer
