Glossary

  • CTA: Call to Action – a prompt encouraging the shopper to complete a specific action.
  • ROI: Return on Investment – the gain or loss from an investment relative to its cost.
  • AOV: Average Order Value – the average dollar amount spent each time a customer places an order.
  • LTV: Lifetime Value – the total revenue expected from a customer throughout their relationship with your business.
  • CTR: Click‑through Rate – the percentage of people who click a link out of the total who see it.
  • FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions – a list of common questions and their answers.
